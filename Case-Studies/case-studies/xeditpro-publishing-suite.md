## Background

Publishers today are under increasing pressure to **shorten time-to-market, improve product quality, and increase internal productivity**. Achieving these goals requires greater automation, modernized workflows, and improved operational efficiencies.

Many publishers also face additional challenges such as:

- Reducing manual editing intervention
- Improving author and editor satisfaction
- Supporting new digital product formats
- Handling multiple data output requirements

Modern publishing workflows are heavily technology-driven. However, **true digital transformation requires more than simply adopting new technologies**. A successful platform must deliver measurable value — enabling publishers to produce content **better, faster, and at lower cost**, while also adapting to emerging publishing requirements.

With the scalability of **cloud computing** and the rapid advancement of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Machine Learning (ML)**, publishers now have the opportunity to significantly improve editing and production workflows.

---

## Solution

**XEditPro Publishing Suite** is an AI-powered digital publishing platform designed to modernize and automate the entire publishing workflow.

Built through decades of collaboration with leading publishers, **diacriTech** developed XEditPro as a unified platform that provides all the tools required for advanced digital content production.

Powered by AI and ML, XEditPro introduces intelligent workflow automation that significantly enhances publishing efficiency.

Key capabilities include:

- AI-driven workflow automation
- Automated content processing and validation
- End-to-end digital publishing management
- Improved quality control across the publishing value chain
- Reduced production costs and turnaround time

The platform enables publishers to gain greater control over operational pain points while improving overall **quality, efficiency, and scalability**.

---

## AI-Powered Pre-Editing and Copyediting

The **pre-editing module** automates several traditionally manual publishing tasks.

Automated capabilities include:

- Content clean-up and formatting
- Data conversions
- Style sheet application
- Consistency checks
- Link validation
- Reference verification

The system also integrates with external scholarly databases to validate references and metadata, including:

- Crossref
- PubMed
- ORCID
- FundRef

Additional automation features include:

- DOI insertion during reference validation
- Rule-based copyediting aligned with publisher style guides
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) based grammar and spelling checks

These automated processes significantly reduce **copyediting turnaround time while maintaining high editorial standards**.

---

## Web-Based Proof Handling Platform

XEditPro includes a **web-based proof management system** that enables seamless collaboration across authors, editors, and production teams.

Key features include:

- Collaborative authoring environment
- Advanced track-change and revision management
- Author query management
- Instant generation of PDF proofs
- EPUB generation after revisions
- Multi-channel product delivery

Supported output formats include:

- Print PDF
- Web PDF
- HTML
- XML
- EPUB
- Microsoft Word

The platform also supports **structured content editing**, including:

- References
- Mathematical equations
- Scientific formulas
- Metadata generation throughout publishing stages

By using a **single-source publishing workflow**, the platform simplifies complex editing processes and reduces production delays.

## Case Example

A leading academic publisher required composition services for a **400-page higher education textbook** featuring:

- Four-color page layouts
- Multiple boxed content elements
- Side-by-side figures
- Complex tables
- Extensive mathematical equations

Using **XEditPro Publishing Suite**, the production team was able to complete the project with **only two revision cycles**.

### Results

- Production time reduced by **45 days**
- Automated generation of:
  - EPUB
  - XML
  - Word outputs
- Maximum automation of **index generation**

This implementation demonstrated how AI-enabled publishing workflows can significantly improve both **speed and production efficiency** while maintaining editorial quality.
